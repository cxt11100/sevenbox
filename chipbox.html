How to use SevenBox

Your private music sketchpad. You own every track you make. Built for you by Grok — offline, no accounts, nothing uploads unless you export.

NotesClick the gray rows at the top. Drag sideways to change note length.
PatternsNumbered boxes under the grid rearrange the song structure.
LoopPurple bar = loop region. Drag the ends to cover more bars.
DrumsBottom channel row is drums. Rows above = melody & harmony.

Keyboard

Space play/pause · Z undo · Y redo · C/V copy/paste · 0–9 pattern · Arrows move selection

Live multiplayer

Run the SevenBox server, click Create room, send friends the site link + room code. Everyone edits the same song (last change wins if you clash). Works on the same Wi‑Fi, or over the internet with a tunnel.
